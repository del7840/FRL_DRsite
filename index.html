ABOUT FORMULA RACING LEAGUE

Formula Racing League has been created by a group of sim racing enthusiasts. The motto is: Sim Racing At Its Very Best!. We always aim at providing a high quality, seamless and friendly experience for our members.

Our main sim platform is Assetto Corsa Competizione on PC. We started back in summer 2021 and with the help of The SimGrid hosting platform we evolved to the top. We are the most followed host on The SimGrid, being integral part of the community, and proudly also part of the annual Grand Finals qualifiers.

Our ACC PC seasons consists of an annual GT3 endurance series ( 2 classes ) and a weekly GT3 sprint series ( 5 classes ). In the off season we host a variety of unique one-off events, such as the Donkey Kong Mountain or Flat Out Eau Rouge.
